Who We Are

At JE Tech Hub, we are driven by a single, powerful mission: to connect education and technology through innovative and practical tutoring that enhances tech skills for individuals and communities alike.
Our goal is not just to teach technology but to inspire a deep, hands-on understanding that empowers learners to become proficient and confident in their technical abilities.

Bridging Education & Technology

We believe that technology education should be accessible, practical, and transformative. Our approach combines theoretical knowledge with hands-on practice to ensure our students are not just learning, but truly understanding and applying their skills.

Our Mission

In today's fast-paced world, technology is at the heart of nearly every industry. At JE Tech Hub, we aim to bridge the gap between education and the evolving tech landscape.


We understand that learning technology can seem daunting, which is why we focus on creating an environment that nurtures curiosity and fosters growth. Whether you are a beginner looking to start your journey in the tech field or an experienced professional aiming to upskill, our mission is to provide you with the tools, guidance, and confidence you need to succeed.

Our Vision

making meaningful impact in the tech industry and beyond." Whether you're an entrepreneur looking to scale, a tech enthusiast eager to explore new frontiers, or someone seeking to upskill, we offer the knowledge, tools, and mentorship to help you thrive in the ever-evolving digital landscape.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming a leading technology training center

2018

Started as JE Gadgets

Initially focused on smartphone repair and diagnostics, we trained 28 individuals, many of whom launched their own repair businesses.

2022-2023

NITDA Partnership

Partnered with NITDA in Kogi & Niger States, training 1,000–1,400 people across a variety of digital and technical skills.

2023

Rebranded to JE Tech Hub

Expanded to offer full IT and software development courses
Moving beyond repairs to include advanced IT and software development courses. we offer hands-on training with real-world equipment.

Present

Innovation Focus

With over 30 graduates with plans for an innovation lab


Our focus includes expanding our community outreach, forming strategic partnerships, and creating an innovation lab to foster local tech entrepreneurship.
